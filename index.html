Physics Laboratories

Online physics courses need online laboratories. Here we are developing tools for optics laboratories. Students will be able to move components on an optical breadboard, align the components, trace a He-Ne laser beam through the system, and make measurements. The app is under development, and you can explore the first steps below.

Experiment 1: Reflection and Refraction

Align components, trace a laser beam, and measure refraction indices.
